The Mara reveals different secrets at different hours. Morning drives catch predators on the hunt and the plains bathed in golden light. Afternoon drives follow the herds as they move toward water. Full-day drives take you deeper — packed lunch under an acacia tree, crossing territories most visitors never reach.

Our guides grew up in this landscape. They read the grass, the sky, the behaviour of a single bird — and they'll show you what you'd never see alone.
