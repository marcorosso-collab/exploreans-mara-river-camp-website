Some meals are best eaten without walls. A bush breakfast after an early morning drive — fresh fruit, eggs, coffee, the sounds of the plains waking up around you. A lunch spread beneath a canopy of acacia, with nothing but grassland in every direction.

And then there are sundowners — cold drinks on a ridge or riverbank as the day's last light turns everything amber. These are not events. They are simply how things are done here.
